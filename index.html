Our Story

Preserving Lost Arts

Maison Merle was born from a fascination with vintage French sewing techniques discovered in forgotten pattern books and estate sale treasures. We became captivated by the meticulous craftsmanship of an earlier era—when every seam was finished with care, every buttonhole was hand-sewn with precision, and every garment was built to last a lifetime.

Today, we preserve these lost arts by applying traditional construction methods to contemporary silhouettes. Each piece honors the heritage of French couture while serving the modern woman who values authenticity, quality, and timeless style.

Our Craftsmanship

Every Maison Merle piece is distinguished by three signature elements that set our garments apart from contemporary fashion—details that speak to those who understand true quality.

French Seams

Our signature French seams create a clean, couture-quality finish that's beautiful inside and out. This time-intensive technique ensures durability while eliminating raw edges for a refined interior construction.

Functional Pockets

We believe luxury shouldn't sacrifice functionality. Our deep, carefully engineered pockets are seamlessly integrated into each design, providing genuine utility without compromising the garment's silhouette.

Hand-Sewn Buttonholes

Each buttonhole is meticulously hand-sewn using traditional techniques, creating a distinctive finish that's both durable and beautiful. This hallmark of fine tailoring requires hours of skilled work per garment.
