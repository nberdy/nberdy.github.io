Poem: Bonus expectations

Oh frabjous day! Today is bonus day!
When all results of my past year's hard toil
Do find themselves reflected in my pay!
The seas of my finances need not roil.
But what is this my paystub shows to me?
The number seems too small to be alright.
It seems I've been paid far less than my fee
Which makes my careful budgeting too tight.
Alas! Alak! I cannot pay my rent!
I have to default on my many loans.
I regret all the money that I've spent
Between loud sobbing, sniveling, and moans.
Self-torturously I recheck my account
And find within my bank the right amount!

In case it needs saying, none of the sonnets are biographical.
